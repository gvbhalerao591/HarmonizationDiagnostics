2025-12-15 16:16:16,132 — INFO — [SECTION] Longitudinal Data Diagnostic Report Introduction
This report provides diagnostic analyses for longitudinal data collected across multiple batches. Longitudinal data involves repeated measurements from the same subjects over time, which introduces additional considerations for batch effects and variability. The following diagnostics will be performed:
Mixed effects model with a subject-specific random term to show the additive effect,
 A batch-wise variance comparison for the scaling effect,
 Within-subject variability (coefficient of variation, percentage difference),
 Subject order consistency across subjects and batches (Spearman correlation),
 Cross-subject variability and preservation of biological effects (e.g., age, diagnosis, etc.).
